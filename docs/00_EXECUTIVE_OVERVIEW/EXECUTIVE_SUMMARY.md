# Executive Summary
- **Current State:** The platform contains substantial intellectual property in transactional analytics, database concurrency controls, and sequence serialization (~90% reusable assets).
- **Target State:** Shift from static historical analytics to forward-looking predictive risk intelligence and interactive AI advisory services.
- **Gap Analysis:** Scoring algorithms are retrospective and lack forecasting capability; configurations are hardcoded.
- **Recommended Actions:** Establish the dynamic Policy Engine; train XGBoost risk models; build the Gemini-based Copilot chat endpoint.
- **Priority:** Critical
- **Risk:** Medium (limited timeline for ML model validation)
- **Dependencies:** Policy Engine design
- **Expected Outcome:** High-impact business intelligence served securely with minimal implementation overhead.
