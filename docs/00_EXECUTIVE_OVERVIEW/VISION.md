# Vision and Product Strategy
- **Current State:** The system serves as a back-office analytics reporter for distributor sales and receipt delays.
- **Target State:** Econiq as the Operating System for Business Health, Commercial Intelligence Infrastructure, and Merchant Credit Risk Engine.
- **Gap Analysis:** Core backend does not support forward default probabilities or strategy recommendations.
- **Recommended Actions:** Implement collections prioritization, churn prediction, and dynamic credit limit optimization hooks.
- **Priority:** Medium
- **Risk:** Low
- **Dependencies:** ML models accuracy
- **Expected Outcome:** Clear positioning of Econiq as a next-generation predictive credit engine.
